About the reviewer

Sebastian Dröge is a free software developer currently working with Centricular Ltd. He has been involved for more than 10 years with the GStreamer project, a cross-platform multimedia framework. He also contributes to various other projects, such as Debian, GNOME, Rust, and WebKit. While finishing his master's degree in computer science, he started working as a contractor on free software and continues to do so to this day.
